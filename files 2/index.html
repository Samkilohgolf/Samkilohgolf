Your Coach

Meet Sam Kiloh

Sam joined the Professional Team at Deeside Golf Club in Aberdeen in 2022 and completed his traineeship through the PGA in 2024. He has competed in many of the mini tours around Europe and has been instrumental in growing the renowned Junior Section at Deeside Golf Club.

In 2024, Sam was appointed PGA Professional & Head of Junior Development.

  • Strike the ball more consistently
  • Eliminate destructive misses
  • Build a swing you can trust under pressure

My Philosophy

Most golfers don't need more information — they need clear feels they can repeat, practice that leads to real change, and confidence in what they're doing. That's exactly what I provide.
